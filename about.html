About Me

Welcome! I am Sunny Pramod, a dedicated Talent Acquisition Specialist with expertise in US staffing. My mission is to bridge the gap between organizations and top-tier talent using data-driven recruitment strategies and generative AI technologies.

With over 2 years of experience in the tech industry, I specialize in executive search, contract recruitment, and staff augmentation. I employ diverse sourcing strategies, utilizing social media, job boards, and professional networks to build qualified candidate pools. My expertise includes leveraging platforms like LinkedIn Recruiter and Boolean Search techniques to identify and attract top technical talent.

I have extensive experience recruiting for various technical roles including Software Engineers, Architects, Database Developers, Web Developers, and Project Managers. I'm skilled in handling different employment types (W2, C2C, 1099) and visa statuses (H1B, EAD, GC).

Certified in Administrative Human Resources and Requirements Elicitation & Analysis, I bring a structured approach to talent acquisition. My focus remains on active sourcing, talent pipeline management, and fostering strong relationships with both hiring managers and candidates.

Beyond recruitment, I am passionate about leveraging AI and automation to enhance hiring processes. I continuously strive to innovate and improve the HR-tech landscape through cutting-edge technologies and data-driven strategies.
